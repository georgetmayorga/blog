PKG.CAT

PKG.CAT is an app that lets people bundle together a collection of links, attachments, and information about themselves to share. A great use case would be a speaker at a conference who wants to bundle their slides, some articles, and a bit about themselves into an email.

Gems used: Paperclip, Griddler, AWS, Bourbon, Neat

George-Bot

I use twitter to find a lot of information and follow some great accounts, but never used it to tweet. I was also interested in learning the Twitter API. With that in mind, I built a twitter-bot that retweets accounts I follow every hour on the :40. Here's the simple rake task that gets it done.
